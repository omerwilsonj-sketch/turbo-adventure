# Echelon Form YouTube Ads Strategy
> **Objective:** Drive sign-ups across all three tiers (Go, Core, VIP) by reaching busy professionals and fitness enthusiasts through targeted YouTube advertising.

---

## 3. Campaign Structure

### Campaign Hierarchy

```
Echelon Form YouTube Ads
├── Brand Awareness (Broad)
│   ├── Targeting: Interest + keywords (broad match)
│   ├── Bid strategy: CPM (viewability focus)
│   └── Budget: 20% of total
│
├── Core Tier Acquisition (Busy Professionals)
│   ├── Targeting: Productivity/business keywords + time-scarcity signals
│   ├── Bid strategy: CPV / Target CPA
│   ├── Budget: 40% of total
│   └── A/B test: Script 1 variations
│
├── VIP Tier Acquisition (High Performers)
│   ├── Targeting: Luxury/wealth interests + C-suite job titles
│   ├── Bid strategy: Target CPA (higher ACoS acceptable)
│   ├── Budget: 25% of total
│   └── A/B test: Script 2 (45s vs 30s cut)
│
└── Go Tier Acquisition (Fitness Enthusiasts)
    ├── Targeting: Fitness interest audiences + workout keywords
    ├── Bid strategy: Target CPA / Enhanced CPC
    ├── Budget: 15% of total
    └── A/B test: Script 3 (20s vs 15s cut)
```

### Retargeting Strategy

| Audience | Trigger | Ad Creative |
|---|---|---|
| **Watched 50%+ of awareness ad** | 50% view through | Retarget with testimonial ad |
| **Visited pricing page, didn't convert** | Page visit | Offer $10 off first month or free coaching call |
| **Started checkout, abandoned** | Add to cart / checkout start | 3-step reminder sequence: Value prop → Social proof → Urgency |
| **Past members who cancelled** | Subscription end | Win-back offer: "We adjusted the program — here's what's new" |
| **Engaged on Instagram/TikTok** | Pixel events | Cross-platform sequential messaging |

## 4. A/B Testing Plan

### Test 1 — Hook Style (Busy Professionals)
- **Variant A:** Problem-first hook ("You keep skipping the gym...")
- **Variant B:** Stat hook ("73% of professionals say 'no time'...")
- **Variant C:** Transformation hook ("I transformed my body in 30 minutes a day...")
- **Winner metric:** View rate, click-through rate to pricing

### Test 2 — Video Length (All Tiers)
- **15s cut** vs **30s cut** vs **60s cut** for each script
- **Winner metric:** Cost per conversion by length

### Test 3 — CTA Placement
- **Variant A:** CTA at end of video only
- **Variant B:** CTA at 50% + end
- **Winner metric:** Click-through rate

### Test 4 — Thumbnail Testing
- **Variant A:** Person looking exhausted (problem)
- **Variant B:** Person looking energized (solution)
- **Variant C:** Before/after split screen
- **Winner metric:** Click-through rate, watch time

### Test 5 — Offer Framing (Core Tier)
- **Variant A:** "$49/month — no contracts"
- **Variant B:** "30-day money-back guarantee"
- **Variant C:** "Join 500+ professionals already transforming"
- **Winner metric:** Conversion rate, cost per acquisition

## 6. Creative Production Notes

### Visual Style Guidelines
- **Color palette:** Dark backgrounds (professional, premium feel) with energetic accent colors
- **On-screen text:** Large, bold, minimal — readable on mobile
- **People:** Real-looking professionals in workout + business settings (not overly airbrushed)
- **Music:** Upbeat but not aggressive — motivational, modern
- **Filming locations:** Office setting, home gym, hotel room, outdoor

### Lower-Third Templates
- Name + Title + Company for testimonial-style ads
- "Managing Director, Goldman Sachs"
- "Founder, 3x Exits"

### End Screens
- Thumbnail image of app
- "Download on iOS/Android"
- "Start Your 90-Day Transformation — $49/mo"

## 7. Compliance & Policy Notes

- All claims ("lose 12 lbs in 90 days") must include typical results disclaimer
- Fitness disclaimers: "Consult your physician before beginning any exercise program"
- Avoid before/after claims that are not representative of typical users
- Include privacy policy link in ad description
- Ensure offer terms are clearly stated
