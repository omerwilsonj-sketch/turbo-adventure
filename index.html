FAQ

Common Questions

How long are the workouts really?

Every workout is designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less. Our HIIT and strength circuits are structured for maximum metabolic response in minimal time. You can complete them at home with minimal equipment or at the gym.

What if I travel frequently for work?

Echelon Form is built for your lifestyle. The app includes hotel-friendly workouts, bodyweight-only options, and nutrition strategies for dining out. Your coach can adjust your program on the fly — just send a message.

Do I need a gym membership?

No. While gym access expands your options, we have full programs that require only a pair of dumbbells or even just your bodyweight. The framework adapts to your environment, not the other way around.

What's the difference between Go, Core, and VIP?

Echelon Form Go ($14.99/mo) gives you self-guided access to the complete 12-week workout library, nutrition guidelines, and exercise demos. It's perfect if you're self-motivated and want the framework without coaching.

Echelon Form Core ($49/mo) adds full app access, weekly group coaching calls, macro tracking, and community chat. It's our most popular tier for professionals who want accountability and structure.

Echelon Form VIP ($249/mo) includes everything in Core plus a dedicated performance coach, custom macro programming, weekly 1-on-1 video check-ins, and direct messaging. Built for high-performers who want maximum results in minimum time.

How is this different from other fitness apps?

Echelon Form isn't a generic fitness app — it's a complete transformation system designed specifically for high-performing professionals. Every element, from the 30-minute workout structure to the accountability framework, is optimized for people who demand elite results on a compressed schedule.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your membership at any time with no hidden fees. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied with your experience.
